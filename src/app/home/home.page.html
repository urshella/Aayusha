Benefits of Ceylon Cinnamon

Cinnamon contains many nutrients such as Calcium, Iron, Magnesium & Vitamin A to name a few. It is primarily made up of Cinnamaldehyde, which scientists believe is the compound responsible for most of cinnamon's powerful medicinal effects on human health and metabolism. Our concoction Naturally, Cinnamon captures the real essence of Cinnamon and all its nutrients in its purest form in an easy to drink beverage.
